Maggi

Description

Maggi is a very popular isntant noodle. Here's what we need to make it.

Ingredients

  • A packet of maggi (contains noodles and masala)
  • Water

Steps

  1. Boil 250mL of water.
  2. Remove a little bit of water. Put the noodle and pour the water until the noodle barely submerges.
  3. Let it cook in the heat until the noodle cake softens.
  4. Put the masala and let it cook for a minute or two.
  5. Turn off the heat, let it cool down a bit and enjoy.
